Guy Aridor

PhD Candidate

Columbia University

I am a fifth-year Economics PhD student at Columbia University in New York. My research focuses on understanding issues in the digital economy.

Before my PhD, I was a software engineer. Most of the engineering work I did was focused around distributed data processing, backend web development, and product experimentation. I wrote and still maintain several open-source software libraries focused on enabling product experimentation.
